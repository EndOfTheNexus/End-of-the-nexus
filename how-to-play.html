How to Play

End of the Nexus is a dark action RPG where you fight enemies, beat bosses, unlock modes, and improve your hero.

Controls

  • Move: `WASD` or arrow keys
  • Attack: `Space`
  • Talk / Interact: `E`
  • Block: `Shift`
  • Flip: `B`
  • Jump Ability: `J`

Main Modes

  • Story Campaign: travel through quests, reach the fortress, and defeat the boss.
  • Boss Rush: fight boss after boss in a row.
  • Zombie Apocalypse: survive the overrun village and stop the underworld threat.
  • Survival Mode: face endless enemies and try to last as long as possible.
  • Training: fight riders that get tougher each wave.
  • Multiplayer: team up or battle another player in a room.

How to Get Stronger

  • Beat quests and bosses to grow your stats.
  • Buy weapons, dragons, and upgrades in the store.
  • Use Premium Ticket rewards if you unlock them.
  • Try different player looks and rider style in Edit Player.

Goal

Keep getting stronger, beat harder bosses, unlock more content, and become a legend in End of the Nexus.
